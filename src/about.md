# Our Mission & Privacy

ssh-vpn was born out of a desire for extreme simplicity and uncompromising privacy. It is developed and maintained by open-source advocates striving to equip every internet user globally with a reliable, untrackable shield.

## No Corporate Surveillance

Traditional consumer VPNs require installing their bloatware certificates while silently logging diagnostic data. **ssh-vpn flips this equation.**
By treating a simple SSH connection as a native system network interface, you cut out the middlemen and the corporate VPN providers entirely.

- **You Own the Node:** Because ssh-vpn communicates directly with an SSH server of your own choosing, no unauthenticated third parties handle your data.
- **DPI Defeat:** SSH traffic blends universally to bypass Deep Packet Inspection targeting normal VPN protocols.
