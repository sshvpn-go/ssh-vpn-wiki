Last Updated: April 12, 2026

Our subscription service provides you with advanced features, including high-speed nodes, unlimited bandwidth, and premium support.

## 2. Automatic Renewal

### 2.1 Renewal Timing
Your subscription will automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle (monthly, quarterly, or annually) unless you cancel it at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

### 2.2 Payment
Payment will be charged to your platform account (e.g., Apple ID, Google Play, or other vendor's account) at the confirmation of purchase. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current billing period.

The price of the subscription is displayed within the app at the time of purchase. We reserve the right to change prices, and we will notify you in advance of any price changes that affect your existing subscriptions.

## 4. Cancellation and Management

### 4.1 How to Cancel
You can manage your subscriptions and turn off auto-renewal through your specific store account settings:
- **Apple (iOS/macOS)**: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.
- **Google Play (Android)**: Google Play Store > Profile Icon > Payments & Subscriptions > Subscriptions.
- **Other Stores (Huawei/Xiaomi/etc.)**: Open the respective App Store > Profile/Me > Payments & Billing or Subscription Management.

### 4.2 Deleting the App
**Please Note**: Deleting the app does not automatically cancel your subscription. You must manually cancel it through your account settings as described above.
