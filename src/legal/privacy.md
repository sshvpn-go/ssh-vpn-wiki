# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

**EasyConnect SSH** ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
We collect minimal personal information necessary to provide the service:
- **Email Address**: Used for account registration and communication.
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely by third-party payment providers (Apple App Store, Google Play Store). We do not store your credit card details.

### 1.2 Technical and Usage Data
To ensure service quality and security, we may collect:
- **Device Information**: Model, OS version, and unique device identifiers.
- **Connection Statistics**: Timestamps of connections and amount of data transferred. **We do not collect or log your browsing history, traffic content, or IP addresses of websites you visit.**

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
- Authenticate your account and manage subscriptions.
- Respond to your inquiries and offer customer support.
- Protect against fraudulent or illegal activities.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:
- **Service Providers**: Third-party vendors who provide analytics, payment processing, or infrastructure services (e.g., Google Analytics, Cloudflare).
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our rights, we may disclose information to law enforcement or regulatory authorities.

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
- **Access**: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction**: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Deletion**: Request that we delete your personal data.
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.
- **Do Not Sell**: (California residents) We do not sell your data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@sshvpn.org.

## 6. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, or country. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

## 7. PIPL Compliance (China)

If you are a resident of mainland China, we comply with the Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL). We minimize data collection and ensure that your personal information is handled with a high level of security. If we transfer data across borders, we do so in accordance with applicable Chinese laws.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 10. Contact Us
