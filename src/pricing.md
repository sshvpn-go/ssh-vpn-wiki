Easy Connect SSH remains 100% free if you use your own self-managed server:
- **Core Engine**: Fully Open-Source (MIT).
- **Deployment**: Follow our **[Server Guide](/guide/server-usage)**.
- **Support**: Peer-to-peer discussions are hosted on GitHub.
